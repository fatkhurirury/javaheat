INTRODUCING

The Eco-Grading System.
The uncompromising standards.

Charcoal has always been sold by trust alone — a fuel without a standard. JavaHeat changes that. The Eco-Grading System is the first formal framework that scores charcoal across four eco-friendly criteria before a single piece is packaged.

  1. G1

    Ultima

    Full supply-chain traceability, verified origin, and responsible production practices with no contribution to deforestation.

  2. G2

    Plus Value

    ≈ 50% raw materials originate from verified and traceable sources, supporting reduced environmental impact and responsible production.

  3. G3

    Ungraded

    Not meeting any minimum environmental and production eco-friendly standards. Untraceable sourcing and unverified origin.
